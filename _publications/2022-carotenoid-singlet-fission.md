This work investigates the mechanism of singlet fission in carotenoids using a polyene model system. Using theoretical electronic-structure analysis, the study elucidates how polyene-like electronic structures can support correlated triplet-pair formation and provides insight into the role of electronic structure in singlet fission processes in carotenoid systems.
